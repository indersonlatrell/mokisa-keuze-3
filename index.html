Our Story

Where Ancient
Tradition Heals

Mokisa is Suriname's first Traditional Maroon Wellness Centre — a sacred space where age-old Afro-Surinamese healing rituals meet contemporary care. Our therapists are trained in authentic Maroon techniques, using natural herbal extracts, steam, and skilled hands to restore balance and vitality.

From detoxifying Sweti steam baths and Dampu herb wraps to deep therapeutic Ali Skin massages and holistic body scrubs — every treatment is rooted in botanical knowledge passed down through generations.
